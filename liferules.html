Guidelines For Life:

1. Season your behaviour with class.
2. Listen more than you talk. Know more about your audience than they know about you.
3. Keep some cards under the table at all times.
4. Consider that you are worth being heard.
5. Time and energy are in scarcity. Spend them on people or projects with intention.
6. You cannot avoid becoming unreasonable. Recognize irrationality. Always behave with class.
7. What you've done is an indicator and not a definition of who you are.
8. Cherish complements internally. Consider critisisms.
